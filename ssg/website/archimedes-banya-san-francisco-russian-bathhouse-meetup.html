Diverse Bathing Facilities

At the heart of Archimedes Banya are its exceptional bathing facilities, designed to cater to various preferences and provide a comprehensive wellness experience.

Russian Sauna (Parilka)

The crown jewel of Archimedes Banya is its two Russian saunas, known as parilki. These saunas offer a unique experience with temperatures ranging from 170Â°F to 210Â°F and humidity levels around 30-40%. The centerpiece of each sauna is a massive 22,000 lb stove covered in handmade ceramic tiles from Russia, designed to provide a soft and pleasant heat. With 16-foot high ceilings and seating at different heights, guests can choose their preferred temperature level for the ultimate detoxifying experience.

Turkish Hammam Steam Room

For those who prefer a more humid environment, the Turkish Hammam steam room offers nearly 100% humidity at temperatures below 140Â°F. This space is perfect for calming the senses and escaping the stresses of daily life. The heated benches and aromatized steam create a dream-like state of relaxation, ideal for skin rejuvenation and hydration.

Finnish Dry Sauna

Contrasting with the humid environments, the Finnish dry sauna provides a hot and dry experience with temperatures above 220Â°F and humidity below 5%. The California cedar wood infuses the air with a comforting aroma, helping to relieve muscle strain and joint stiffness.

Cold Water Plunge

For the adventurous, the cold water plunge offers an exhilarating contrast to the heat of the saunas. This ice-cold pool provides a breathtaking experience that invigorates the cardiovascular system and contributes to the euphoric emotional state often associated with traditional banya practices.

Warm Water Pools and Jacuzzis

Between sauna sessions, guests can enjoy the warm water swimming pool and Jacuzzis. These family-friendly areas offer a refreshing interlude and a chance to socialize with friends.

Unique Treatments and Services

Archimedes Banya offers a range of traditional and modern treatments to enhance the bathing experience.

Venik Platza

One of the most authentic Russian banya experiences available is the venik platza treatment. This involves being gently struck with bundles of birch branches by a skilled platza master while lying in the hot sauna. This traditional practice is believed to improve circulation and provide a deep, invigorating massage.

Massages and Spa Treatments

The banya offers a variety of massages, scrubs, wraps, and herbal and mineral baths to complement the sauna experience. These treatments can be customized to individual preferences and are perfect for those looking to pamper themselves or recover from physical activities.
