Why A.T. O'Keeffe's is a fun option when searching for things to do near me with friends and family

A.T. O'Keeffe's is a vibrant Irish pub and bourbon club located at 911 Boylston Street in Boston's bustling Back Bay neighborhood. Renowned as the oldest sports bar in America, this historic venue carries on the legacy of the beloved McGreevy's, offering a lively and welcoming atmosphere perfect for meeting up with friends. With daily hours from 11 a.m. until 2 a.m., it provides ample opportunity to enjoy great food, carefully curated drinks, and engaging events. The menu features comforting Irish and American favorites such as Shepherd's Pie, Fish & Chips, Burgers, and rich Truffle Mac & Cheese. For those who appreciate fine spirits, A.T. O'Keeffe's boasts an extensive bourbon selection alongside a range of local and international beers. The pub is also known for its late-night pizza service from 10 p.m. to 2 a.m., catering to night owls looking for a hearty bite. Regular events such as whisky tastings and themed parties add to the dynamic social experience. The blend of nostalgic charm, quality food and drink offerings, and a lively crowd make A.T. O'Keeffe's a top choice for casual gatherings and spirited nights out in Boston’s Back Bay.
