Why Rock Paper Scissors Collective is Ideal for Meetups

A Hub for Creativity and Collaboration

RPSC's mission is to foster creativity and collaboration within the local community. Since its establishment in 2004, it has been a vital resource for East Bay artists and community members, providing a space to exhibit, sell, teach, learn, and collaborate.

Diverse and Engaging Activities

RPSC offers a wide range of activities, including low-cost creative workshops, curated gallery exhibitions, and community events. This variety ensures that there's something for everyone to enjoy and participate in.

Community-Focused Atmosphere

The collective is committed to strengthening local communities and encouraging sustainable creative practices. Its non-hierarchical, consensus-based organizing model creates a welcoming and inclusive environment for all.

Supporting Local Artists

RPSC is dedicated to promoting and supporting local artists by providing a platform to showcase and sell their work. This commitment not only enriches the community but also offers unique and meaningful experiences for visitors.

Offerings and Activities

Workshops

RPSC offers a diverse range of workshops for people of all ages and skill levels. These workshops cover various artistic disciplines, including:

  • Ceramics: Hands-on classes where participants can learn the basics of pottery and create their own ceramic pieces.
  • Crochet: Engaging workshops for learning the art of crochet, from beginner-friendly projects to more advanced techniques.
  • Zine Making: Workshops focused on creating zines, offering participants a platform to express themselves through writing, art, and collage.
  • Various Art Classes: The specific workshops may vary, so checking the website or contacting them is crucial.

Gallery Exhibitions

RPSC hosts curated and open-call gallery exhibitions, showcasing the work of local artists. These exhibitions provide a platform for artists to gain exposure and offer visitors the opportunity to discover new and exciting art.

Community Events

RPSC organizes a variety of community events throughout the year, including:

  • First Friday Art Walk: RPSC is a founding member of Oakland Art Murmur and participates in the monthly First Friday Art Walk, where galleries and art spaces open their doors to the public.
  • The 23rd St. Fair (now Oakland First Fridays): RPSC co-founded this hallmark event, demonstrating its long-standing commitment to supporting the local arts scene.
  • Special Events: RPSC hosts various special events, such as Valentine's Day workshops, film screenings, and performance art shows.

Retail of Independently Produced Goods

RPSC's retail space features a curated selection of independently produced goods, including art, craft items, and zines. This offers visitors the opportunity to support local artists and find unique treasures.
